<div class="wp-block-uagb-advanced-heading uagb-block-c5054e2a"><p class="uagb-heading-text">This dimension compares how legends differ across cultures while maintaining similar themes. For instance, the &#8220;Woman in White&#8221; archetype appears in various forms: the &#8220;Kuchisake-onna&#8221; in Japan, &#8220;La Llorona&#8221; in Mexico, and &#8220;Pontianak&#8221; in Southeast Asia. </p></div>



<div class="wp-block-uagb-advanced-heading uagb-block-72f3541d"><p class="uagb-heading-text">It analyzes how local history, religion, and gender roles shape these narratives. By studying these variations, we uncover universal human fears—such as maternal grief, infidelity, or social exclusion—translated into unique cultural metaphors. </p></div>
