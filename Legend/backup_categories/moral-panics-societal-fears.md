<div class="wp-block-uagb-container uagb-block-e22a1dc9">
<div class="wp-block-uagb-advanced-heading uagb-block-c5054e2a"><p class="uagb-heading-text">Many urban legends are manifestations of &#8220;Moral Panics&#8221;—generalized fears that someone or something threatens the values of society. Tales of organ harvesting, poisoned Halloween candy, or kidnapping rings in white vans reflect deep-seated mistrust in strangers and institutional decay. </p></div>



<div class="wp-block-uagb-advanced-heading uagb-block-72f3541d"><p class="uagb-heading-text">This dimension explores how these legends are weaponized during periods of social change. It analyzes the role of the media in amplifying these myths, often leading to real-world hysteria and legal repercussions despite a lack of evidence.</p></div>
</div>
